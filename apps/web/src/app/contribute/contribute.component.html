How you can help contribute

Suggest an App

Have you come across an app that has been particularly helpful for managing a disability or improving accessibility? Share it with us! By suggesting apps, you help us ensure our directory remains comprehensive and up-to-date. Simply fill out our suggestion form with details about the app, and our team will review it for inclusion in the directory. Your suggestions help us serve a broader range of needs, making valuable tools more accessible to everyone who needs them.

Fund an App

Support developers who are creating innovative solutions to accessibility challenges by funding their projects. Through our platform, you can contribute financially to the apps you believe in. This not only helps maintain and enhance these apps but also encourages the development of new solutions. Look for the funding options on app pages and choose to make a difference.

Review an App

Your feedback is invaluable! Review apps you have used to help others in the community make informed decisions. Whether it’s a thumbs up or constructive criticism, your reviews shape the future of the apps in our directory and help developers improve. To leave a review, simply visit an app’s page on our site and submit your thoughts and ratings.

List an App

Are you a developer of an accessibility-focused app? List your app in our directory to reach a wider audience. Our platform offers a unique space to showcase your app to users who can truly benefit from it. Fill out our listing form with details about your app, and we’ll take care of the rest. Listing your app helps you connect with the community and get valuable user feedback to further refine your application.
